Live Formulas
Instantaneous EMF
iThe voltage at any given moment during rotation. Varies as a cosine function of the phase angle.
ε = ε₀ cos(ωt)
ε = 4.50V · cos(0.00) = 4.50 V
Peak EMF
iε₀ = N·B·A·ω — Peak voltage depends on number of turns (N), field strength (B), coil area (A), and rotation speed (ω).
ε₀ = N · B · A · ω
ε₀ = 60 · 0.30T · 0.025m² · 10.0 = 4.50 V
Peak Current
iMaximum current flowing through the circuit. Equals peak EMF divided by resistance: I₀ = ε₀ / R.
I₀ = ε₀ / R
I₀ = 4.50V / 4.50Ω = 1.00 A
